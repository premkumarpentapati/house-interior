Concept Suite

Five directions. One language.

All five stay inside Modern Warm Minimalist — but each has a distinct material temperature, budget posture, and emotional register.

Concept 01Budget ●●●○Mediterranean–Indian

Sienna Haven

Cream walls, light oak, terracotta accents, handcrafted pottery moments. Warmest and most “welcoming at the door.”

Cream
Beige
Terracotta
Oak
Brass

Paint: Royale Ivory 0315 + Terracotta-N 427 accent niches
Furniture: Curved linen sofa, cane chairs, solid oak tables
Lighting: Ceramic/brass pendants, 2700–3000K
Best for: Living, dining, pooja, guest room

Pros

  • Instantly warm & hospitable
  • Strong Indian craft dialogue
  • Photogenic evening glow

Cons

  • Terracotta can feel dated if overused
  • Needs disciplined accent control

Oat & Oak

The purest soft-minimal expression: oat walls, pale oak joinery, linen, travertine, almost no colour noise. Boutique calm.

Taupe

Paint: Royale Windsor Cream 0989 / Ivory 0315; Royale Play Marmorino accent
Furniture: Low linen sofa, oak platform beds, fluted wardrobes
Lighting: Sculptural linen pendants + cove, CRI 90+

Pros

  • Most timeless & resale-friendly
  • Best cost-to-luxury ratio
  • Easiest maintenance visually

Cons

  • Can feel plain if textures skip
  • Needs excellent lighting layers

Lime & Linen

Limewash walls, soft sage accents, olive branch energy. Hotel-spa serenity with a gentle outdoor connection.
