Existing house analysis

Floor-plan PDFs and current photographs were not present in the repository at build time. This section documents working assumptions and the analysis method so designs remain adaptable when your files arrive.

Important: Designs below use a carefully constructed working duplex layout typical of contemporary Indian duplex homes (~2,500–2,700 sq.ft total). When your First Floor / Second Floor PDFs and photos are uploaded, room dimensions, window positions, and service stacks will be recalibrated — concept language and material system remain valid.
Light

Prioritise daylight

Living, dining, and master assumed on brighter façades. Sheer linen curtains; avoid heavy drapery that kills warmth.

Circulation

Foyer–stair spine

Stair treated as a sculptural calm void, not a leftover. Visual continuity between floors via shared palette.

Structure

No major demolition

Design works within existing walls. Wet areas stay near stacks. Storage thickened into joinery, not new rooms.
