Lighting Design

Light is half the interior.

Professional layered lighting: ambient, task, accent. Warm living temperatures. Dimmable everywhere it matters.

Ambient

3000K

General soft wash via cove, wide-beam downlights (beam 60°+), and indirect uplight. Target ~150–200 lux living.

Task

3500K

Kitchen counters, study desk, vanity mirror sides. Higher clarity without going cold blue.

Accent

2700K

Pendants, floor lamps, art washers, stair drama. Evening atmosphere.
