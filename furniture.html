Ergonomics

Seat heights suited to Indian living habits; dining chair comfort over sculptural pain; bed height easy for elders.

Storage

Vertical wardrobes, kitchen pantry pull-outs, living console drawers — clutter has a home so surfaces stay calm.

Finishes

Matte woods, soft fabrics, brass accents. No high-gloss piano finishes.
