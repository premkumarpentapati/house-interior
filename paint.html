Asian Paints Specifications

Paint is our quiet luxury engine.

Official Asian Paints products wherever applicable. Texture finishes replace expensive cladding while keeping a premium read.

Always confirm shade codes on a physical Asian Paints swatch under your room’s daylight before bulk order. The same name can vary slightly across product lines.

Application strategy

  • 70%Flat Royale matt cream/ivory for calm continuity
  • 20%Royale Play textures on focal walls only
  • 10%Accent colour niches (terracotta / honey / sage)
  • CeilingsAlways light warm white — never accent ceilings

Cost vs cladding

A skilled Royale Play Marmorino wall typically costs a fraction of full-height marble or veneer cladding while delivering comparable “premium first impression” in photographs and evening light.
