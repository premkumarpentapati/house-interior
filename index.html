Project Brief

A home that feels calm at noon and golden at dusk.

Inspired by boutique hotels, Scandinavian warmth, Japanese restraint, and contemporary Indian living — never cold, never flashy, never over-decorated.

01 · Atmosphere

Warm, soft, spacious

Oat, cream, oak, linen, and gentle brass. Luxury from proportion, light, and texture — not marble overload.

02 · Discipline

Soft minimalism

Curated negative space. One focal moment per room. Easy to maintain. Designed to age gracefully for a decade+.

03 · Reality

India-ready detailing

Asian Paints Royale & Royale Play, local laminates, quartz, hardware, and furniture available in the Indian market.

Design Statement

“Luxury should come from proportions, lighting, materials, and thoughtful details — not excessive decoration.”

This proposal does not chase trends that age quickly. It uses current 2025–26 warm minimal research as inspiration, then adapts ideas to this duplex’s circulation, light, and family practicality.
