Final Recommendation

Choose Concept 02 — Oat & Oak
with selective Sienna accents.

After scoring all five concepts on beauty, warmth, function, cost, practicality, family comfort, storage, maintenance, longevity, and resale — Oat & Oak is the clearest whole-house winner.

Primary directionConcept 02

Oat & Oak

Soft minimal continuity across both floors. Royale Ivory / Windsor Cream walls, pale oak joinery, linen, travertine moments, brushed brass, layered warm lighting.

Accent borrowFrom Concept 01

Sienna hospitality moments

Use terracotta vessels, one niche (Terracotta-N 427), and craft pottery only in living / pooja / entry — never as a full-house colour flood.

Why this wins

Clear reasoning, not trend-chasing.

Matches your brief exactly

Modern, warm, soft minimal, premium, calm, easy to maintain — without heavy luxury, excess marble, or wood cladding overload.

Cost-intelligent luxury

Royale + Royale Play textures deliver the expensive look where it counts. Budget concentrates on joinery, lighting, and hardware.

Whole-house cohesion

One palette climbs the stair with you. Guests feel continuity; family feels rest. Bedrooms stay S→N as required.

Ages slowly

Oat, oak, linen, and brass are not seasonal Instagram colours. Resale photography stays desirable for years.
