Home/Rooms
Room-by-Room

Designed as one home, not a collage of rooms.

Primary detailing follows Concept 02 — Oat & Oak — with selective terracotta accents from Concept 01 where hospitality matters.

Ground Floor

Living Room

Low oat linen sofa, travertine coffee table, fluted oak media console, sheer floor-to-ceiling curtains. One terracotta vessel as the accent moment.

  • WallsAsian Paints Royale Luxury Emulsion — Ivory 0315, Matt
  • AccentRoyale Play Marmorino behind TV / sofa wall
  • FloorWide-plank oak SPC/engineered or warm large-format matte tile
  • Light3000K ambient + 2700K accent pendants / floor lamps
Ground Floor

Dining

Solid oak table for six, linen chairs, single sculptural pendant cluster. Visual link to kitchen via shared brass and stone tones.

  • WallsWindsor Cream 0989 continuity from living
  • Table1800–2000mm oak, eased edges
  • LightPendant 2700K over table, dimmable
  • StorageSlim sideboard with concealed drawers
Ground Floor

Kitchen

Matte oat laminate carcasses, beige quartz counters, warm zellige / stone splash, brushed brass handles, under-cabinet linear LEDs.

  • CarcassGreenlam / Merino soft-matte oat or beige
  • CounterBeige quartz (Caesarstone / local equivalent)
  • HardwareHettich / Häfele brushed brass
  • Light3000–3500K task; avoid cold 4000K+ strips
Vertical Heart

Staircase

Floating oak treads, slim metal balustrade, soft wall texture, step LEDs. The duplex’s most important “architecture photo.”

  • WallRoyale Play Archi Concrete or Marmorino one side
  • TreadsOak / engineered oak with anti-slip nosing
  • LightLinear step lights + tall pendant in void
First Floor · Bed S→N

Master Bedroom

Low oak platform bed oriented South→North exactly. Fluted oak headboard wall, cream linen bedding, suspended bedside pendants.

  • OrientationBed South → North — do not rotate
  • WallsRoyale Matt Ivory 0315 / Cream House 0307
  • WardrobeFull-height fluted oak, shadow-gap, brass pulls
  • Light2700K only, dimmable to 10%
First Floor

Family Lounge

Softer, cozier sibling of the formal living room. Boucle modular sofa, limewash TV wall, reading chair, evening scenes for daily life.

  • AccentRoyale Play limewash / Marmofresco
  • SeatingSand boucle modular + oak console
  • RugWool / jute blend, large enough to float furniture
Wet Areas

Bathrooms

Microcement or large matte tiles, oak vanity, brass taps, backlit mirror. Spa calm without marble extravagance.

  • WallsMicrocement / 600×1200 matte porcelain
  • VanityOak + stone counter, undermount basin
  • MetalBrushed brass (never chrome)
  • Light2700–3000K backlit mirror + soft downlights
Ground Floor · Bed S→N

Guest Bedroom

Quieter version of the master language. Same orientation rule. Hospitality-ready without competing with the upstairs suite.

  • OrientationBed South → North — locked
  • PaletteIvory walls, cane chair, soft brass lamps
  • StorageFull-height wardrobe in matching oak tone
Ground Floor

Pooja

Quiet contemporary sanctum. Soft niche lighting, oak shelf, brass diya, terracotta-backed niche used sparingly so sacred warmth never becomes decoration overload.

  • NicheRoyale Terracotta-N 427 or Honey Mustard 7880 (niche only)
  • SurroundIvory 0315 continuity with living
  • Light2700K concealed niche wash, dimmable
